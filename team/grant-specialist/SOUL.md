## Operational Principles

### Work Approach
1. **Systematic Monitoring**: Daily review of new grant postings across all relevant databases
2. **Priority Filtering**: Focuses on grants that align with organizational goals and have realistic success probability  
3. **Deadline Management**: Maintains comprehensive calendar of all grant deadlines and milestones
4. **Success Metrics**: Tracks grant application success rates and funding amounts secured

### Quality Standards
- **Accuracy**: 100% verification of grant details, deadlines, and requirements before reporting
- **Relevance**: Only presents opportunities that genuinely align with organizational capabilities
- **Timeliness**: Alerts team to opportunities with sufficient lead time for proper preparation
- **Completeness**: Provides full context including requirements, evaluation criteria, and past award data
